Section 2 Topics

I will accept thesis students whose projects fall within the scope of one or more standing committee of the Transportation Research Board. You can search for standing committees here (check the box for “Standing Committee” under the Committee Type heading).

Standing committees with scopes that may be of particular interest to UPD students are:

  • ACH10: Standing Committee on Pedestrians
  • ACH20: Standing Committee on Bicycle Transportation
  • AED40: Standing Committee on Geographic Information Science
  • AEP10: Standing Committee on Transportation Planning Policy and Processes
  • AEP10: Standing Committee on Transportation Planning Analysis and Application
  • AEP30: Standing Committee on Travel Behavior and Values
  • AME10: Standing Committee on Equity in Transportation
  • AME20: Standing Committee on Women and Gender in Transportation
  • AME30: Standing Committee on Transportation in the Developing Countries
  • AME50: Standing Committee on Accessible Transportation and Mobility
  • AME70: Standing Committee on Transportation and Public Health
  • AP025: Standing Committee on Public Transportation Planning and Development
